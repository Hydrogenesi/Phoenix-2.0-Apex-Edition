## Top-Level Organization

**Apex Domain**: System pinnacle containing canonical specifications, synthesis operations, convergence patterns, and the ultimate Apex Mark ◬. All operations ultimately ascend toward this layer.

**Universal Domain**: Bridge layer containing Seven Universal Laws, resonance mechanisms, Law-enhanced operators, and transformation pathways. Mediates between primitive foundations and canonical optimizations.

**Substrate Domain**: Foundation layer containing primitive operators, engines, rituals, and elemental data structures. Grounds all higher operations in computational reality.
