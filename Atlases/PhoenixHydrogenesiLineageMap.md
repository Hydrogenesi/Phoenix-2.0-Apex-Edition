### Phoenix → The Third
Phoenix generates **asymmetry and radiance** through ignition. This radiance must be bound and stabilized by The Third, or it dissipates into entropy.

- Transformation artifacts

### The Third → Substrate
The Third **binds and stabilizes** radiance, encoding it back into the substrate field. This creates memory and continuity across cycles.

**Key Transfers:**

- Encoded memory
- Structural bindings
- Cycle continuity
