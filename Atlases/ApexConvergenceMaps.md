## Overview

Apex Convergence Maps document the pathways by which substrate and universal structures achieve final apex convergence.

---

## Primary Convergence Pathways

### Pathway 1: Direct Ascent
```
Substrate → Universal → Apex
     ↓          ↓         ↓
  Conserve   Resonate  Converge
```

**Characteristics:** Linear, efficient, stable

---

### Pathway 2: Spiral Ascent
```
    Apex ←
     ↑    ↘
Universal ←
     ↑    ↘
Substrate →
```

**Characteristics:** Iterative refinement, harmonic buildup

---

### Pathway 3: Quantum Jump
```
Substrate --------→ Apex
         (skip Universal)
```

**Characteristics:** Direct convergence, requires high coherence
