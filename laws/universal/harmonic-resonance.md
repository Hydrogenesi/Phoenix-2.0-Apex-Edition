## Formal Statement

**Alignment amplifies power.**

When components align in phase, frequency, and intention, their combined effect exceeds the sum of their individual contributions. Resonance creates coherent amplification—constructive interference that magnifies capability.

## Mathematical Expression

```

Resonant condition: φ₁ ≈ φ₂ ≈ ... ≈ φₙ

  A_total ≈ ∑ᵢ Aᵢ  (coherent sum)

Resonance occurs when:

  θ₁ = θ₂ = ... = θₙ  (phase alignment)
```

## Detailed Explanation

Harmonic Resonance is the phenomenon where aligned components achieve amplified effects. This isn't mystical—it's the same principle that allows a child on a swing to build momentum through properly timed pushes, or allows a singer to shatter glass through sustained frequency matching.

### What Resonance Means

**Frequency Alignment**: Components operating at compatible frequencies reinforce rather than interfere with each other.

**Phase Synchronization**: Components whose cycles align in phase create constructive interference, amplifying the total effect.

**Intentional Coherence**: When purposes and goals align, efforts reinforce rather than conflict or waste energy on internal friction.

**Emergent Amplification**: The resonant whole is genuinely more powerful than the sum of parts, by a factor that scales with the number of aligned components.

### Practical Implications

**For System Design**: Design components to have compatible "frequencies"—similar data formats, complementary interfaces, aligned purposes. Misaligned components waste energy fighting each other.

**For Rituals**: Ritual power comes from aligning multiple elements (words, gestures, intent, timing, symbols) into resonance. Each element reinforces the others, creating amplified effect.

**For Teams**: Teams achieve peak performance when members align on goals, methods, and timing. Misalignment creates friction; alignment creates resonance.
