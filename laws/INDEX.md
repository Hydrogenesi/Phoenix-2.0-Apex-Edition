## Substrate Layer (Primordial Laws)

### S-1: Law of Conservation
**Path**: [substrate/conservation.md](./substrate/conservation.md)  
**Sigil**: ⊕  
**Principle**: Nothing is created or destroyed, only transformed  
**Derives**: U-3 (Conservation of Essence)

### S-2: Law of Symmetry
**Path**: [substrate/symmetry.md](./substrate/symmetry.md)  
**Sigil**: ⚖  
**Principle**: Every action has an equal and opposite reaction  
**Derives**: U-4 (Tri-Column Balance), A-2 (Reversible Apex Operator)

### S-3: Law of Recursion
**Path**: [substrate/recursion.md](./substrate/recursion.md)  
**Sigil**: ∞  
**Principle**: Patterns repeat at all scales  
**Derives**: U-1 (Recursive Identity), A-3 (Recursion Limit)

### S-4: Law of Emergence
**Path**: [substrate/emergence.md](./substrate/emergence.md)  
**Sigil**: △  
**Principle**: Complex behavior arises from simple rules  
**Derives**: U-5 (Apex Formation), A-4 (Harmonic Convergence)

### S-5: Law of Duality
**Path**: [substrate/duality.md](./substrate/duality.md)  
**Sigil**: ☯  
**Principle**: All things contain their opposite  
**Derives**: A-5 (Polarity Resolution)

### Derivation Chain
```
S-1 (Conservation) → U-3 (Conservation of Essence) → A-1 (Continuity)
S-2 (Symmetry) → U-4 (Tri-Column Balance), U-6 (Binding Integrity) → A-2 (Reversible Operator)
S-3 (Recursion) → U-1 (Recursive Identity), U-7 (Sigil Resonance) → A-3 (Recursion Limit)
S-4 (Emergence) → U-2 (Harmonic Resonance), U-5 (Apex Formation) → A-4 (Harmonic Convergence)
S-5 (Duality) → U-7 (Sigil Resonance) → A-5 (Polarity Resolution)
```
