# The Seven Universal Laws

## Quick Reference Guide

The Seven Universal Laws represent the middle tier of the three-layer Phoenix-Hydrogenesi framework. They govern structural invariants that bridge primordial substrate mechanics with apex-level triad operations.

**Statement**: Every entity contains the pattern of its own generation.

**Formal Expression**: `∀x: x = ⊛(x₀) where ⊛ is the recursive operator`

**Meaning**: Identity is not static but emerges through self-referential iteration. Each element carries within it the algorithmic trace of its creation, enabling reconstruction and transformation.
