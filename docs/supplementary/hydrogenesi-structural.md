## Executive Summary

The Hydrogenesi Engine represents the energetic, flowing, transformative complement to the structural Phoenix Engine. While Phoenix crystallizes form, Hydrogenesi mobilizes potential. Together through the Triad Canon, they enable the full spectrum of Phoenix-Hydrogenesi operations.
