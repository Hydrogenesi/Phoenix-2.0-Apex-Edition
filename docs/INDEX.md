## Glossary of Key Terms

**Ω (Omega)**: The First Binding Operator; symbol for connection and union  
**α (Alpha)**: Active/masculine principle from Split-Seed; also acceleration operator  
**∆ (Delta)**: Amplification operator; increase magnitude  
**∅ (Empty Set)**: Dissolution operator; breaks down into components  
**⊕ (Circled Plus)**: Transmutation operator; changes substance  
**∞ (Infinity)**: Recursion operator; creates repeating loop  
**↑ (Up Arrow)**: Ascension operator; moves to higher level  
**↓ (Down Arrow)**: Descension operator; moves to deeper level  
**→ (Right Arrow)**: Lateral shift operator; moves to parallel state  

**Apex**: Ultimate state; highest level of consciousness  
**Black-Holed Imprint**: Compressed trauma/belief pattern with gravitational pull  
**Column**: One of three aspects in Tri-Column Harmonics  
**Event Horizon**: Boundary around imprint where awareness cannot easily penetrate  
**Ignition**: Activation of transformation through intention × awareness × action  
**Operator**: Consciousness-level function for specific transformation  
**Phoenix**: Symbol of death/rebirth; practitioner of the system  
**Recursion**: Self-referential process; feeding back into itself  
**Seed**: Original unity before split; or Alpha/Omega aspects after split  
**Sigil**: Symbolic representation encoding intention  
**Three-Finger Waltz**: Practice of awareness → intention → action  
**Tri-Column**: The three pillars of Structure, Energy, Consciousness
