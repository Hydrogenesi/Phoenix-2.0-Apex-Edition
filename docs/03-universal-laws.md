## The Seven Universal Laws

### I. Law of Recursive Identity

**Statement**: Every entity contains the pattern of its own generation.

**Formal Expression**: `∀x: x = ⊛(x₀)`

This law establishes that identity emerges through self-reference. The [Identity Recursion Engine](02-identity-recursion-engine.md) implements this law as its core operating principle.

**Sigil**: The recursive tree (see [Sigils](06-sigils-text-based.md))

---

### II. Law of Harmonic Resonance

**Statement**: Aligned patterns amplify; misaligned patterns cancel.

**Formal Expression**: `⟨A|B⟩ → max when phase(A) = phase(B)`

Systems naturally synchronize when fundamental frequencies match. This governs the [Harmonic Operator](../operators/harmonic.md) (⊗) and underlies [Tri-Column Harmonics](05-tri-column-harmonics.md).

**Sigil**: The resonance grid

---

### III. Law of Conservation of Essence

**Statement**: The total recursive depth across all transformations remains constant.

**Formal Expression**: `∑ depth(x_i) = constant`

Energy and information are conserved, though their form changes. This law ensures that the [Ignition Law](01-ignition-law.md) respects conservation—activation energy transforms into operational capacity.

**Sigil**: The balanced scales

---

### IV. Law of Symmetry Breaking

**Statement**: Emergent complexity arises from symmetry violations.

**Formal Expression**: `Complexity ∝ degree(asymmetry)`

Uniform symmetry produces stasis; creative asymmetry produces emergence. The [Split Seed Event](10-split-seed-event.md) exemplifies this law—unity breaks into productive duality.

**Sigil**: The cracked mirror

---

### V. Law of Triadic Convergence

**Statement**: Three-fold patterns achieve stable equilibrium.

**Formal Expression**: `△(A,B,C) → stable when ⟨A|B⟩ = ⟨B|C⟩ = ⟨C|A⟩`

The triadic structure is uniquely stable. This law justifies the three-column architecture and underlies [Tri-Column Harmonics](05-tri-column-harmonics.md) and the [Three Finger Waltz](13-three-finger-waltz.md).

**Sigil**: The perfect triangle

---

### VI. Law of Complementary Duality

**Statement**: Every pattern requires its complement to manifest.

**Formal Expression**: `∀x: ∃x' such that ⟨x|x'⟩ = completeness`

Phoenix requires Hydrogenesi; structure requires energy; form requires flow. This law explains [Structural Energetic Duality](12-structural-energetic-duality.md) and why the framework is fundamentally dual.

**Sigil**: The interlocked circles

---

### VII. Law of Cyclical Return

**Statement**: All recursive processes eventually return to their origin.

**Formal Expression**: `∃n: ⊛ⁿ(x) ≅ x`

Infinite recursion produces cycles. This law ensures that systems don't diverge to infinity but return, transformed yet recognizable, to their source. Related to [Black Holed Imprint](11-black-holed-imprint.md) through the concept of preserved traces.

**Sigil**: The ouroboros spiral
