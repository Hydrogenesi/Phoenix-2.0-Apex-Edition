## Error Handling

### Binding Errors

**Error**: Engine state conflict  
**Cause**: Phoenix, Hydrogenesi, or The Third states contradictory  
**Resolution**: Resolve conflicts at engine level, re-attempt binding

**Error**: Essence infusion failure  
**Cause**: Layer 13 essence incomplete or corrupted

**Error**: Apex lattice unstable  
**Cause**: Binding incomplete or geometric error  
**Resolution**: Stabilize lattice, complete binding, retry release

**Error**: Invariants violated  
**Cause**: Crown Invariant conditions not met  
**Resolution**: Repair violations, verify all checks, retry release
