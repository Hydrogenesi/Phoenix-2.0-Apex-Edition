## Substrate Geometric Forms

### ⟨○⟩ — The Circle
**Geometry:** Perfect circle  
**Properties:**
- 360° symmetry
- Infinite points equidistant from center
- No beginning or end

**Symbolic Meaning:** Wholeness, conservation, eternity

---

### ⟨⚌⟩ — The Mirror Line
**Geometry:** Vertical axis of symmetry  
**Properties:**
- Reflection axis
- Divides space into dual halves
- Perfect bilateral symmetry

**Symbolic Meaning:** Duality, reflection, balance

---

### ⟨∞⟩ — The Lemniscate
**Geometry:** Figure-eight curve  
**Properties:**
- Two loops connected at crossing point
- Self-intersecting
- Continuous flow

**Symbolic Meaning:** Infinity, recursion, eternal return

---

### ⟨△⟩ — The Triangle
**Geometry:** Three-sided polygon  
**Properties:**
- Stable structure (three points)
- Simplest 2D closed form
- Sum of angles = 180°

**Symbolic Meaning:** Stability, emergence, foundation

---

### ⟨⚯⟩ — The Division/Union
**Geometry:** Split circle or vesica piscis  
**Properties:**
- Two circles intersecting
- Creates almond-shaped center
- Unity through overlap

**Symbolic Meaning:** Split/merge, duality, creation
