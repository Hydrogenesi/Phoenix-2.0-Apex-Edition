## The Five Apex Sigils

### Sigil of Apex Continuity
**Symbol:** ⟦≈⟧  
**Operator:** `ApexContinuity()`  
**Meaning:** The unbroken wave — that which flows without gap  
**Properties:**
- Represents seamlessness
- Signifies smooth convergence
- Encodes continuity

**Invocation:** "Let all flow as one"

**Properties:**

- Signifies lossless transformation
- Encodes bidirectionality

**Invocation:** "What ascends can descend"
