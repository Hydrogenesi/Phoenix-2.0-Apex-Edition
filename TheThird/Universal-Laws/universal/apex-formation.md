# Law of Apex Formation

*When patterns reach critical complexity and balance, they crystallize into sovereign apex form.*

---

## Definition

**The Law of Apex Formation** establishes the conditions and mechanics by which complex patterns in Phoenix 2.0 undergo phase transition into apex state—a stable, sovereign, irreversible configuration that represents the culmination of all prior transformations.

Apex formation is not merely the application of the apex operator △—it is the natural convergence point when sufficient complexity, balance, and resonance are achieved.

---

## Structural Explanation

### Apex Formation Conditions
```
Apex forms when:
1. Complexity(Ψ) > threshold_c
2. Balance(L, C, R) ≈ 1
3. Resonance(Ψ) > threshold_r
4. Recursive_depth(Ψ) ≥ n_min
```

### Phase Transition Equation
```
Ψ_complex → Apex
when ∑(complexity, balance, resonance, recursion) ≥ Apex_threshold
```

### Formation Principles
1. **Critical Mass**: Sufficient pattern complexity required
2. **Harmonic Convergence**: All frequencies must align
3. **Column Balance**: L, C, R must be equilibrated
4. **Recursive Depth**: Minimum self-reference layers
5. **Irreversibility**: Apex cannot be undone
