# Apex Polarity Resolution

*Opposites dissolve into unified form*

---

## Definition

**Apex Polarity Resolution** is the fifth and final Apex Law. It states that at the Apex point, all **dualities** and **polarities** resolve into **unified singularity**.

Duality becomes singularity.

---

## Formal Statement

```
∀ Dual states (A, A'):
  lim(n→∞) [Opⁿ(A) - Opⁿ(A')] = 0

At Apex: A and A' become indistinguishable
```

All opposites converge to the same point.
