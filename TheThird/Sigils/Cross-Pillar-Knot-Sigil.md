## Geometric Elements

### Central Symbol: ⊕⊞
Combined Phoenix (⊕) and Hydrogenesi (⊞) symbols representing **symmetric dual binding**.

### Horizontal Axis
The **symmetry axis** connecting left (Phoenix) and right (Hydrogenesi) corridors.

### Double Diamond Structure
Two sets of four diamonds (left and right) representing the **dual input fields**.

### Crossing Mark: ×
The **binding cross** where both corridors meet and fuse.
