# Law of Duality

**Structural Role:** Fundamental Polarity

## Statement

All states exist in superposition; form and void dance in eternal balance.

## Meaning

- Every state contains its opposite.
- Creation and destruction are two faces of the same transformation.
- Duality is the primordial tension from which triads later emerge.
