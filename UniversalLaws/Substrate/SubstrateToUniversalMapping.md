**Substrate Layer Documentation**

## Overview

This document describes how substrate operators map to and feed into universal layer operators.

## Mapping Table

### Conservation → Recursive Identity
**Substrate:** `Conserve(x) → x'`  
**Universal:** `Identity(x) → x.self()`

Conservation of substrate properties enables recursive self-identity at the universal layer.

---

### Symmetry → Binding Integrity
**Substrate:** `Mirror(x) → x*`  
**Universal:** `Bind(x, y) → bound(x, y)`

Symmetry operations at substrate enable structural binding at universal layer.

---

### Recursion → Recursive Identity
**Substrate:** `Recurse(x) → Recurse(Recurse(x))`  
**Universal:** `Identity(x) → x.self()`

Substrate recursion provides the foundation for universal recursive identity.

---

### Emergence → Harmonic Resonance
**Substrate:** `Combine(x, y) → z`  
**Universal:** `Resonate(x, y) → harmonize(x, y)`

Emergent combination at substrate enables harmonic resonance at universal layer.

---

### Duality → Tri-Column Balance
**Substrate:** `Split(x) → (x₁, x₂)` | `Merge(x₁, x₂) → x`  
**Universal:** `StabilizeTriad(substrate, universal, apex) → balanced_structure`

Substrate duality operations provide the foundation for tri-column balance.
