## Operator Precedence

When multiple operators are applied:

1. **Conserve/Preserve** — Always first
2. **Identity** — Establish self-reference
3. **Resonate** — Create harmony
4. **Bind** — Form connections
5. **StabilizeTriad** — Achieve balance
6. **ConvergeApex** — Prepare for apex
7. **SigilOperate** — Execute symbolic operations
