## âš–ï¸ Universal Laws

Phoenix 2.0 is governed by five universal laws that ensure consistency and reversibility:

### 1. [Law of Conservation](./laws/conservation.md)
> *What is created must be accounted for; nothing emerges from void without trace.*

All transformations preserve harmonic balance. Energy and information are neither created nor destroyed, only transformed.

### 2. [Law of Symmetry](./laws/symmetry.md)
> *For every operator, there exists a mirror; every action has an equal and opposite resonance.*

Every operator has a dual form. Symmetry ensures that all transformations can be reversed or reflected.

### 3. [Law of Recursion](./laws/recursion.md)
> *What descends must return; patterns repeat across scales until apex is reached.*

Recursive operators fold back upon themselves, creating self-similar structures that compound across iterations.

### 4. [Law of Emergence](./laws/emergence.md)
> *Complexity arises from simple rules; apex forms appear when operators harmonize.*

When operators combine in ritual sequences, emergent behaviors manifest that transcend their individual properties.

### 5. [Law of Duality](./laws/duality.md)
> *All states exist in superposition; form and void dance in eternal balance.*

Every state contains its opposite. Creation and destruction are two faces of the same transformation.
