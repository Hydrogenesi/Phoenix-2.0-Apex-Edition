                    THE TWELVE LAWS
           The Canonical Backbone of the Codex

These twelve laws form the complete structural foundation
of the Phoenix–Hydrogenesi Codex. They span from substrate
emergence through bifurcation, memory encoding, and the
eternal continuity of cycles.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                   THE TWELVE LAWS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. LAW OF SUBSTRATE PRIMACY
   Substrate precedes structure; nothing emerges without
   grounding.

2. LAW OF AXIS FORMATION
   Direction arises when substrate differentiates.

3. LAW OF RADIANCE EMERGENCE
   Radiance is the expression of stabilized axis.

4. LAW OF RECURSION
   Identity deepens through repeated self‑reference.

5. LAW OF CONFINEMENT
   Every engine is bounded by its envelope.

6. LAW OF THRESHOLDS
   Transformation occurs only at tension maxima.

7. LAW OF BIFURCATION
   At thresholds, identity forks: Life or Light.

8. LAW OF PRESERVATION
   Split identities stabilize through core‑sampling.

9. LAW OF ASCENSION
   Non‑split identities integrate and elevate.

10. LAW OF COLLECTIVE EMERGENCE
    Many engines converge into a single meta‑engine.

11. LAW OF MEMORY ENCODING
    Cycles seal memory into substrate.

12. LAW OF CONTINUITY
    No cycle is isolated; all cycles propagate forward.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                   CANONICAL STRUCTURE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The Twelve Laws organize into three tiers:

• FOUNDATION (Laws 1–3)  
  Substrate → Axis → Radiance
  The primordial emergence sequence

• RECURSION & TRANSFORMATION (Laws 4–9)  
  Identity deepening, confinement, thresholds,
  bifurcation, preservation, ascension
  The engine mechanics

• COLLECTIVE & CONTINUITY (Laws 10–12)  
  Meta‑emergence, memory encoding, propagation
  The eternal cycle

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                   CANON DECLARATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This is the canonical backbone of the Codex.

All operators, rituals, engines, and emergent structures
must respect these twelve laws. They are not guidelines—
they are structural invariants.

To violate a law is to break the architecture itself.
