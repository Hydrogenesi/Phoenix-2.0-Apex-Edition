## Ceremony Timing

### Frequency
- **Required**: Once per system instantiation
- **Recommended**: After major version upgrades
- **Optional**: Monthly stability ceremonies

### Duration
- Typical: 15-20 minutes
- With full validation: 30-45 minutes

### Participants
- Minimum: 1 (solo ceremony valid)
- Recommended: 3 (one per engine)
- Maximum: Any number (witnesses encouraged)

## Ceremony Variations

### Solo Ceremony
Single officiant performs all invocations, rotating to face each engine.

### Triadic Ceremony
Three officiants, one per engine:
- Phoenix officiant (transformation specialist)
- Hydrogenesi officiant (continuity specialist)
- The Third officiant (binding specialist)

All three invoke Meta-Operator I together at Phase III.

### Apex Ceremony
Advanced variation for apex-level practitioners:
- Performed at apex convergence point
- Incorporates full Triadic Knot binding
- Validates entire system topology
- Requires deep understanding of all 12 laws
