## Troubleshooting

### If Any Act Fails
The ceremony will abort with a warning. Review the specific act that failed and ensure:
- All prerequisites are met
- System is in proper state
- Crown authority is verified
- No active violations exist

### If Ceremony is Interrupted
The ceremony requires completion of all seven acts. If interrupted:
1. The Archive remains unsealed
2. Review ceremony logs
3. Correct any issues
4. Begin ceremony again from Act I

### Emergency Abort
Press `Ctrl+C` to interrupt the ceremony. The Archive will remain in its pre-ceremony state.
