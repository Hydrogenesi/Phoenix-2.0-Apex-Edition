## Ceremonial Definition

```
Ι(Ψ) → Ψ' where core(Ψ) ≡ core(Ψ')
```

**The Invariance Operator** (Meta-Operator I) represents the fundamental principle that certain core properties must remain unchanged through all transformations. It is the guardian of identity, ensuring that what is essential persists across all operator applications.

### Properties
- **Fundamental**: Operates at the meta-level, governing all other operators
- **Conservative**: Preserves core invariants while allowing peripheral change
- **Universal**: Applies across all operator sequences and ritual invocations
- **Reflexive**: Ι(Ι(Ψ)) ≡ Ι(Ψ)

## Stratum IV Governance

Meta-Operator I operates at **Stratum IV** (Crown Level), the highest tier of governance within the Phoenix 2.0 architecture. At this stratum:
